Dear Virtuleap Hackathon Judges,
Thank you for your consideration. Adit offers a full suite of exploratory data analysis features for the HTC Vive. Please explore with one of the provided datasets or upload your own. If you don't have a Vive available, you can see a demonstration of the interactive data analysis in this brief video.
If adit is selected as the hackathon winner, here are some exmaples of directions I would explore for VR data analysis as a productivity tool:
  • Save State: save and share your created data visualizations
  • Telepresence: invite others to join you in VR or on monitors to collaborate on or present visualizations
  • 4D: map 4 positional variables and view a projection of the 4D space in VR
  • Plot Types: create line, ellipsoid, stacked volume plots, and more
  • Model Diagnostics: examine residuals of multiple predictors simultaneously to discover interaction effects
  • A-Frame Components: extract more functionality from Adit to share with the WebVR community, like Super Hands
